About

When I'm not programming I enjoy reading, running, and playing video games. I am currently attending Oregon State University online pursuing a post baccalurete degree in Computer Science. My first degree was a BBA from Western Michigan University with a concentration in management. After graduating with my first degree I didn't really know what I wanted to do, but I found an interest in technology and worked through the materials on Free Code Camp. I loved it, but I still want to learn more so I enrolled at Oregon State and started freelancing as a web developer and designer. I update this page as I learn more and add projects as I complete them so feel free to explore my site and check back often. If you would like to get in contact with me you can check out my various social media pages, or fill out the form at the bottom of this page.
